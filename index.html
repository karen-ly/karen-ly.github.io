Get to Know Me

Hello! My name is Karen Ly, and I'm a sophomore studying Computer Science at the University of Southern California. As a first-generation college student growing up in a predominantly Vietnamese hometown, I hold pride in my unique culture, especially in the powerful immigrant stories of individuals, like my own parents, who moved from Vietnam to the U.S. for a better life and started over from nothing. Guided by my core values of hard work, learning, and family, I hope to become an understanding and compassionate software engineer to address socio-economic disparities such as the lack of quality education and healthcare in communities like my own.

As Maya Angelou says, "When you learn, teach. When you get, give." And that's my life goal.
